About Us

At New Satya Narayan Welding Works, we specialize in hydraulic press jobs, fabrication, machining, bending, and precision plate rolling. Our expertise includes manufacturing industrial components such as Reducers, Pipes, Fabricated Spheres and more. Quality is the pillar of our success. Each project is executed with precision and durability in mind. All welds are X-ray Quality (RT) and Ultrasonically Testable (UT) for superior reliability.

Our Services

Hydraulic Press Jobs

High-pressure hydraulic press jobs for industrial applications, offering precise force control and consistency. We handle large-scale metal forming, punching, and shaping tasks, ensuring every component meets exact specifications and industry standards.

Fabrication Jobs

Comprehensive fabrication services for industrial structures, supports, and machinery components. Our skilled team uses advanced techniques to cut, assemble, and weld materials with precision, delivering durable, high-quality structures customized to your project requirements.

Machining Jobs

Precision machining of metal components to exact tolerances using advanced equipment. We provide milling, turning, drilling, and finishing services to produce parts that perfectly fit your machinery, ensuring smooth operation and long-lasting performance.

Bending Jobs

Professional bending services for industrial materials. We provide seamless pipe bending, angle bending, channel bending, square bar bending, and custom shapes. Each bending task is performed with precision to ensure correct angles, curves, and shapes suitable for industrial assemblies, maintaining structural integrity.

Custom Components

Custom components designed and fabricated according to your specific requirements. We specialize in creating unique industrial parts, transitional pieces, reducers, and more. Each product undergoes strict quality checks, including X-ray and ultrasonic weld testing for superior reliability.

Welding Jobs

High-quality MIG, TIG, and Arc welding services for custom industrial assemblies and repairs. Ensuring strong, durable, and precise welds for all your projects.
